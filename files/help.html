Home Page

1.Introduction

Application is played by using your sport knowledge and skill.You can pick your own team made up of real players for Cricket.Your team earns points based on your chosen players'performance in the real-life matches.It's time to showcase your skill and go for glory!

Follow these easy steps to get started:


Select A Match :

Select any of the upcoming matches from any of the current or upcoming cricket series.

Create Your Team :

Use your sports knowledge and showcase you skills to create your Dream team.

Follow the Match :

Watch the real match and track your fantasy scoreboard. (updated every 3 seconds).


2.Create Your Team:

Step 1: Select a Match

select any upcoming match from the list of ongoing and upcoming cricket series and click on the 'Create Team' button.
Create your Dream team by picking 11 players as per the following combinations.
TEAM STRUCTURE
Player Type Number
WK(Wicket-Keeper) 1
Bat(Batsman) 4
AR(All-Rounder) 2
Bow(Bowler) 4
Total 11

Step 2:Select your Dream team's Captain & Vice Captain

After creating your Dream Team, choose a Captain & ViceCaptain for the team.
Captain-Gets 3x points scored by him in the actual game.
Vice Captain-Gets 2x points scored by him in the actual game.
ONCE A TEAM CREATED YOU CAN NOT MODIFIED.
